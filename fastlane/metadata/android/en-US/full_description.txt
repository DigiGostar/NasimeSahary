Free and open-source Nasime Sahary with Compass/Qibla and Athan, and
Afghanistan holidays, Dari, Kurdish, Arabic, and Pashto support. Also, Supports
Gregorian and Islamic calendars.

Also, works with TalkBack accessibility feature of Android.

https://github.com/ebraminio/DroidPersianCalendar

Please note that the permanent date notification bar can easily be disabled
from preference. Also, it has a DashClock extension and optional Athan
player.
